Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. Spendly is a personal expense tracking application, and we aim to handle your data responsibly and transparently.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account, such as your email address and password. We also store the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may collect limited technical information, such as basic log data, to keep the service running reliably.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Spendly service: to authenticate your account, to store and display your expenses, and to generate the summaries and spending patterns you see. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your financial data for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored so that it is available to you whenever you sign in. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third party services to host the application and keep it operational. Where we use such services, we share only the information necessary for them to perform their function, and we expect them to handle your data with appropriate safeguards. We do not share your personal financial data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will do our best to respond promptly and address your concerns.
